Spaghetti

Ingredients you will need

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Meatballs
  • Pasta Sauce

Steps to make this dish

  1. Heat water in a stock pot until boiling
  2. Once water has reached a boil, place pasta in water
  3. Season and cook the meatballs until reaching an internal temperature of 165
  4. In a pan, combine butter and pasta sauce and simmer
  5. Combine all ingredients and enjoy
