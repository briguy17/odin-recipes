Butter Chicken

Ingredients you will need

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Curry Powder
  • Butter Sauce

Steps to make this dish

  1. Wash and cook your rice
  2. Dice, season and cook the chicken until reaching an internal temperature of 165
  3. In a pan, combine butter and butter sauce and simmer
  4. Combine all ingredients and enjoy
