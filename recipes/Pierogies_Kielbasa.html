Pierogies and Kielbasa

Ingredients you will need

  • Pierogies
  • Onions
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Kielbasa

Steps to make this dish

  1. Melt butter in a pan
  2. Dice, season and cook the kielbasa until reaching an internal temperature of 165
  3. In a pan, combine butter and onions and simmer
  4. Combine all ingredients and enjoy
